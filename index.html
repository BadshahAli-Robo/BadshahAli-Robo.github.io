Companion Robots

Designing expressive, human-friendly study-companion robots such as Pia-the-µRSC and RSC v4.1. My work focuses on motion personality, micro-expressions, motivational behaviour, and accessible interaction systems for learning environments.

Embedded HRI

Developing embedded human-robot interaction systems combining gesture detection, voice input, expressive motion, LEDs, and sensor-driven behaviour. Specialized in real-time robotics using Raspberry Pi Pico and ESP32.

End-to-End Builds

Full hardware–software pipeline: CAD modeling, 3D printing, mechanical assembly, servo calibration, sensor integration, wiring, soldering, and firmware development in MicroPython & C. Focus on reproducible educational robots.
