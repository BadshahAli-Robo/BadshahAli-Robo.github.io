Who I am

I am Ali Badshah, a BSc student in Science & Technology at the University of Tartu with a specialisation in Robotics and Bio-Engineering. I enjoy building friendly robots that sit on desks, react to people and make studying less lonely.

My work combines embedded systems, 3D-printed mechanisms and human-robot interaction. Recently I have been focusing on the Robotic Study Companion (RSC) and Pia-the-µRSC, improving portability, adaptability and cost-effectiveness.

I like to learn by doing: soldering hardware, debugging weird servo behaviour, tuning motion, and documenting everything so others can replicate my builds.

Skills

  • Microcontrollers (Raspberry Pi Pico, ESP32, Raspberry Pi)
  • Python, MicroPython, C basics
  • CAD & 3D printing (SolidWorks / others)
  • Digital signal processing foundations
  • Human-Robot Interaction & companion robots
