SO-101 Leader–Follower Arms

LeRobot Hackathon project.

Built and calibrated one leader and one follower SO-101 robotic arm. The follower replicated the leader’s movements in real-time to perform precise pick-and-place tasks. Demonstrated synchronized motion-following using the LeRobot framework.
