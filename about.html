About Me

Robotics • Embedded Systems • Human–Robot Interaction

Who I Am

I am a Robotics & Bio-Engineering student at the University of Tartu, specializing in companion robots, embedded systems, and expressive study-support platforms such as Pia-the-µRSC and the Robotic Study Companion (RSC). My work combines CAD design, 3D printing, electronics, firmware, and human–robot interaction to build friendly, accessible, and reproducible robotic systems for education and research.

I am deeply interested in creating small, expressive robots that help motivate students, support learning, and bring personality to educational environments through motion and behaviour.

Skills & Tools

  • Companion Robots & HRI
  • Embedded Systems (RPi Pico, ESP32, Raspberry Pi)
  • CAD & 3D Printing (Fusion 360, SolidWorks)
  • Electronics, Wiring & Prototyping
  • Firmware: MicroPython, Python, C/C++
  • Interaction Design & Motion Personality

Current Focus

Building Pia-the-µRSC with improved hardware, CAD redesign, sensor integration, servo optimization, and complete reproducibility-focused documentation. My goal is to create a portable, robust, low-cost study-companion robot that students can build, maintain, and extend.

Education
