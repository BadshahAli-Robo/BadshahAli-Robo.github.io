SO-101 Leader–Follower Arms

LeRobot Hackathon project: motion-replication robotic arms.

Built and calibrated one leader and one follower SO-101 robotic arm. I worked on servo alignment, kinematic mapping, control-signal filtering, and real-time motion replication. The follower arm successfully mirrored the leader's movements to pick and place objects in a live demonstration.

  • Robotic arms
  • Kinematics
  • Motion replication
