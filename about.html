About Me

Robotics • Embedded Systems • Human–Robot Interaction

Who I Am

I am a Robotics & Bio-Engineering student at the University of Tartu, specializing in companion robots, embedded systems, and expressive study-support platforms such as Pia-the-μRSC and the Robotic Study Companion (RSC).

My work combines CAD design, 3D printing, electronics, firmware development, and human-robot interaction to create friendly, accessible, and reproducible robotic systems for education and research.

I am deeply interested in building expressive micro-robots that motivate students, support learning, and bring personality to educational environments through motion and behaviour.

Education

BSc in Science & Technology (Robotics & Bio-Engineering)

University of Tartu, Estonia

Current Focus

Building Pia-the-μRSC with improved hardware, CAD redesign, servo optimization, sensor integration, and reproducibility-focused documentation.

My long-term goal is to create a portable, robust, and low-cost micro companion-robot platform that students can build, maintain, and extend easily.
