About Me

Robotics • Embedded Systems • Human–Robot Interaction

Who I Am

I am a Robotics & Bio-Engineering student at the University of Tartu, working on companion robots, embedded hardware, and expressive study-support robots like Pia-the-μRSC and the Robotic Study Companion (RSC).

My work blends 3D printing, electronics, firmware, and human-robot interaction to create friendly, accessible, and reproducible robotic systems for education and research.

Skills & Tools

  • Embedded Systems (RPi Pico, ESP32, Raspberry Pi)
  • HRI / Companion Robots
  • Electronics & Hardware Prototyping
  • 3D Printing & CAD
  • Python, C/C++, MicroPython

Current Focus

Building Pia-the-μRSC, documenting RSC v4.1 assembly, and designing more portable study-companion robots.
