About Me

Robotics • Embedded Systems • Human–Robot Interaction

Who I Am

I am a Robotics & Bio-Engineering student at the University of Tartu, specializing in companion robots, embedded systems, and expressive study-support platforms such as Pia-the-μRSC and the Robotic Study Companion (RSC).

My work combines CAD design, 3D printing, electronics, firmware development, and human-robot interaction to create friendly, accessible, and reproducible robotic systems for education and research.

I am deeply interested in building small, expressive robots that help motivate students, support learning, and bring personality to educational environments through motion and behaviour.

Skills & Tools

  • Companion Robots & HRI
  • Embedded Systems (Rpi Pico, ESP32, Raspberry Pi)
  • CAD & 3D Printing (Fusion 360, SolidWorks)
  • Electronics, Wiring & Prototyping
  • Firmware: MicroPython, Python, C/C++
  • Interaction Design & Motion Personality

Current Focus

Building Pia-the-μRSC with improved hardware, optimized servo actuation, sensor integration, CAD redesign, and complete reproducibility-focused documentation.

My goal is to build a portable, robust, low-cost companion robot that students can build, maintain, and extend easily.

Education

BSc in Science & Technology (Robotics & Bio-Engineering)
